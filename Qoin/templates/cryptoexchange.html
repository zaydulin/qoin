Licensed VASP in Poland

A VASP (Virtual Asset Service Provider) license is a legal permit required for companies providing virtual (digital) asset services. A VASP license confirms that the company complies with regulatory standards, including AML and KYC procedures, and ensures security,transparency, and user protection.

Qoin+ holds VASP License No. RDWW-1097 issued by the Financial Supervision Commission (KNF), the authorized government agency in Poland.

What a VASP license allows:

  • Virtual asset exchange

    Converting one cryptocurrency to another or to traditional currencies (e.g., USD, EUR).

  • Virtual asset transfer

    Transferring cryptocurrency between users or platforms.

  • Custody

    Providing custody and management services for clients' digital {% static 'site/assets, ensuring their safekeeping.

  • Portfolio Management
