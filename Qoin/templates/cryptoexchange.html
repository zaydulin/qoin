Licensed VASP in Poland
A VASP (Virtual Asset Service Provider) license is a legal permit required for companies providing virtual (digital) asset services. A VASP license confirms that the company complies with regulatory standards, including AML and KYC procedures, and ensures security,transparency, and user protection.
Qoin+ holds VASP License No. RDWW-1097 issued by the Financial Supervision Commission (KNF), the authorized government agency in Poland.
What a VASP license allows:
Virtual asset exchangeConverting one cryptocurrency to another or to traditional currencies (e.g., USD, EUR).
CustodyProviding custody and management services for clients' digital assets, ensuring their safekeeping.
Token Offering ServicesParticipation in organizing initial coin offerings (ITOs) and other financial services related to the issuance of virtual assets.
Virtual asset transferTransferring cryptocurrency between users or platforms.
Portfolio ManagementProfessional management of cryptocurrency portfolios on behalf of clients.
